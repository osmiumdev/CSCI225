Monty Hall Problem Simulator

Thank you for playing the Monty Hall Problem Simulator Game!


Instructions:

You are presented with three doors, two have goats behind them, and one has a shiny new car! When prompted in the lower panel, you may choose which one of three closed doors to "lock-in." After locking in a door, the host will reveal one of the goat doors! Finally, you will be given one last opportunity to either keep your original choice, or to switch to the other, unopened door! After making your decision, all doors will be opened, and the results will be recorded.

Prompt:

Uh oh! Stinky!
